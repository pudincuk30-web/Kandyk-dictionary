Kandyk Sibrisky

Description

A short fused person hiding under the disguise as someone calm and quiet. Often sleeping off his emotions. He knows this though, usually waking up feeling embarassed for being mad over small things. Another side effect of this is that he gets easily angered when in a fight. Which causes him to despise fighting and study support-oriented artz instead. However, when around his close ones, he's often more expressive with his emotions. A common complaint about him is that he tends to throw punches at his friends and cousins whenever he feels annoyed or embarrased.
