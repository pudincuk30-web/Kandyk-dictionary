Kandyk Sibrisky

Description

A short fused person hiding under the disguise as someone calm and quiet. Often sleeping off his emotions. He knows this though, usually waking up feeling embarassed for being mad over small things. Another side effect of this is that he gets easily angered when in a fight. Which causes him to despise fighting and study support-oriented artz instead. However, when around his close ones, he's often more expressive with his emotions. A common complaint about him is that he tends to throw punches at his friends and cousins whenever he feels annoyed or embarrased.

Trivia

  • He's short sighted
  • He's scared of heights
  • All of his attire is stolen from someone else
  • He carries a medical handbook to rehearse everyday

Movesets

Medical Analysis

Roll to analyze the enemy weakpoint for a +2 advantage on an action roll.

Uses careful approach

Prequisites:

  • Target is in the same zone/ in a zone with clear visibility from his own
  • Target is made of organic tissue(plants, meat, anything that is made from cells)

Internal Acelleration

Use fate points to remove a consequence from himself or his allies. Can be used outside battles.

Fate point requirements:

  • 1 point to remove mild consequence
  • 2 points to remove moderate consequence
  • 3 points to remove severe consequence

Architectually-Inclined

Roll to analyze weakpoint of structures OR anorganic enemies for a +2 advantage on an action roll, uses clever approach

Prequisites:

  • Target is in the same zone/ in a zone with clear visibility from his own
  • Target is made of anorganic material(metal, rocks, robots, rotting corpses, etc)

Background

Before the void, he lived in a remote town inside eastern siberia. the zone was unstable, it being a contested area between two countries.

He was adopted by his grandfather after the mysterious dissapearance of his parents. Scared of the same thing happening to him and his grandfather, he swears to develop useful skills to escape and move to another country.
